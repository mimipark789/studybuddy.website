FRENCH REVOLUTION FUN FACTS

1. During the Revolution, approximately 18,000 people were beheaded by the “national razor”, though some estimates run as high as 40,000.

2. The Reign of Terror, during which the Revolutionary government arrested 300,000 suspects, resulting in at least 25,000 deaths.

3. Louis XVI was the first of the royal to go on January 12, 1793, but his execution was only secured by a narrow majority: 361 votes for and 288 against.
